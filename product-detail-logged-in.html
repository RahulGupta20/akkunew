Performance That Delivers

Powered by the cutting-edge 13th Gen Intel Core i7-13700H processor featuring hybrid architecture with 6 Performance-cores and 8 Efficient-cores, the XPS 15 handles demanding workloads with ease. Whether you're editing 4K video, rendering 3D models, or running multiple virtual machines, this laptop delivers consistent, reliable performance.

The NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4050 GPU with dedicated 6GB GDDR6 memory brings hardware-accelerated ray tracing and AI-enhanced graphics to your creative workflow. Experience faster rendering in Adobe Premiere Pro, smoother viewport performance in Blender, and the ability to game at high settings during your downtime.

Visual Excellence

The 15.6-inch 3.5K (3456 x 2160) OLED touchscreen is factory-calibrated for 100% DCI-P3 color accuracy, making it perfect for professional photo and video editing. With true blacks, infinite contrast ratio, and HDR support, every frame comes to life with stunning clarity and vibrant colors.

The touchscreen supports pen input for creative work, while the anti-reflective coating ensures visibility even in bright environments. At 400 nits brightness, you can work comfortably indoors or outdoors.

Built for Professionals

The backlit keyboard with 1.3mm key travel provides a comfortable typing experience for long work sessions. The precision touchpad is 62% larger than previous generations, supporting Windows 11 gestures and multi-finger controls with accuracy.

Quad-speaker design tuned by Waves MaxxAudio Pro delivers immersive sound for video calls, content consumption, and multimedia editing. Dual-array microphones with AI noise reduction ensure crystal-clear audio in online meetings.

Security & Privacy

Windows Hello facial recognition with IR camera provides secure, password-free login. The fingerprint reader integrated into the power button offers an additional biometric security option. TPM 2.0 chip ensures hardware-level encryption for sensitive data.

Physical camera shutter provides privacy when you need it, while Dell Optimizer with ExpressResponse prioritizes your most-used applications for improved performance and responsiveness.

Storage: Yes, the M.2 SSD is user-replaceable and upgradeable. You can upgrade from the included 1TB SSD up to 4TB using standard M.2 2280 PCIe Gen 4 SSDs.

Yes, the Dell XPS 15 fully supports dual 4K external displays through its Thunderbolt 4 ports. Here's what you need to know:

  • Both Thunderbolt 4 ports support DisplayPort 2.0 over USB-C
  • You can connect two 4K displays at 60Hz simultaneously
  • Alternatively, you can connect one 8K display at 30Hz
  • Use a Thunderbolt 4 dock for expanded connectivity options
