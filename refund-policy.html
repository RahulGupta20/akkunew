Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

At Akku Electronics, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions and procedures for requesting refunds on products purchased from our store.

1. Overview

We offer a 14-day return and refund policy from the date of delivery for most products. This means you have 14 days after receiving your item to request a return and refund.

To be eligible for a refund, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You'll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

Our Commitment: We stand behind the quality of our products and are committed to ensuring your complete satisfaction with every purchase.

2. Refund Eligibility Criteria

2.1 Products Eligible for Refund

  • Unopened products: Items that are sealed and unopened in their original packaging
  • Defective products: Items that are defective or not working as described
  • Wrong items: If you received the wrong product
  • Damaged in transit: Products that were damaged during shipping
  • Changed mind: Unopened items within 14 days (subject to 15% restocking fee)

2.2 Required Conditions

For your refund request to be approved, the following conditions must be met:

  • Item must be returned within 14 days of delivery
  • Product must be in original condition with all accessories and documentation
  • Original packaging must be intact (box, manuals, cables, etc.)
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or order number) must be provided
  • Product serial numbers must match the original purchase
  • No signs of physical damage, misuse, or wear
Important: Products with broken seals, missing accessories, or signs of use may not be eligible for a full refund. A restocking fee may apply.

3. How to Request a Refund

3.1 Step-by-Step Process

1

Contact Us

Reach out to our customer service team within 14 days of delivery via:

  • Email: returns@akku.gg
  • Phone: +977 98123412345
  • Online: My Orders > Request Return
2

Provide Information

Submit the following details:

  • Order number
  • Product name and serial number
  • Reason for return
  • Photos of the product (if defective or damaged)
3

Get Approval

Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours and provide:

  • Return authorization (if approved)
  • Return shipping instructions
  • Return address details
4

Ship the Product

Pack the item securely with all accessories and ship it to our return center:

  • Use the original packaging if possible
  • Include all accessories, manuals, and warranty cards
  • Keep the tracking number for your records
5

Inspection & Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Inspection takes 3-5 business days
  • You'll receive email notification of approval/rejection
  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

4. Refund Processing Timeframe

4.1 Processing Times

Stage Timeframe Details
Return Request 24-48 hours Initial review and approval
Shipping to Us 3-7 days Depends on your location
Inspection 3-5 business days Quality check and verification
Refund Processing 5-7 business days From approval date
Bank/Card Processing 5-10 business days Depends on your bank

4.2 Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days to reflect in your account
  • Digital Wallets (eSewa, Khalti): 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 5-7 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer or store credit (your choice)

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds under our standard policy:

Opened Software

Software with broken seals or activated licenses

Hygiene Products

Earphones, headsets with opened packaging

Gift Cards

Activated or partially used gift cards

Custom Built PCs

Custom configured systems (unless defective)

Clearance Items

Products sold as "final sale" or "clearance"

Installed Products

Items that have been installed or modified

Note: Even if a product is non-refundable, it may still be eligible for warranty service or exchange if it's defective. Please refer to our Warranty Policy for more details.

6. Product Return Conditions

6.1 Packaging Requirements

  • Original manufacturer's box with all labels intact
  • All protective packaging materials (foam, plastic wraps)
  • All bundled accessories (cables, adapters, manuals)
  • Warranty cards and documentation
  • Any promotional items or free gifts included

6.2 Product Condition

Condition Description Refund
Like New Sealed/unopened, all accessories intact 100% refund
Opened - Unused Opened but unused, all items present 85% refund (15% restocking fee)
Minor Issues Missing accessories or damaged packaging 50-70% refund (case by case)
Used/Damaged Signs of use or physical damage Not eligible for refund

7. Exchanges

We offer product exchanges in the following situations:

7.1 Exchange Options

  • Defective Products: Exchange for the same model or equivalent
  • Wrong Item Received: Exchange for the correct item at no cost
  • Different Model/Upgrade: Pay the price difference
  • Different Model/Downgrade: Receive refund of the difference

7.2 Exchange Process

The exchange process is similar to returns:

  1. Contact us and specify you want an exchange
  2. We'll arrange pickup of the original item
  3. Replacement will be shipped once we receive the return
  4. No additional shipping charges for defective/wrong items

8. Return Shipping Costs

8.1 Who Pays for Return Shipping?

Reason for Return Shipping Cost Responsible Party
Defective Product Free We cover all costs
Wrong Item Shipped Free We cover all costs
Damaged in Transit Free We cover all costs
Changed Mind Customer pays You cover shipping
Ordered Wrong Item Customer pays You cover shipping

8.2 Prepaid Returns

For defective or wrong items, we provide:

  • Prepaid return shipping label
  • Free pickup service from your location (Kathmandu Valley)
  • Tracking information for your return

9. Damaged or Defective Items

9.1 Receiving a Damaged Item

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  1. Don't accept delivery if the outer packaging is visibly damaged
  2. Document the damage with photos immediately upon opening
  3. Contact us within 48 hours with photos and order details
  4. Keep all packaging for inspection if required

9.2 Fast-Track Process

For damaged/defective items, we offer expedited service:

  • Immediate approval upon verification (no 24-48 hour wait)
  • Option for immediate replacement before returning the defective item
  • Free pickup service within 24 hours
  • Priority refund processing (3-5 business days)
Quality Assurance: All products are tested before shipping. If you receive a defective item, we sincerely apologize and will make it right immediately.
