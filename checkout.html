Return Policy

Our 7-Day Return Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return process.

Return Window

  • You have 7 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return
  • Items must be in original, unused condition with all tags and packaging intact
  • Proof of purchase required (order number or receipt)

Eligible Items

  • Electronics in unopened, sealed packaging
  • Accessories with original packaging and tags
  • Defective or damaged items (warranty applies)

Non-Returnable Items

  • Software, games, or downloadable products (once activated)
  • Opened personal care items or headphones (hygiene reasons)
  • Custom-built or personalized items
  • Sale or clearance items (unless defective)

Return Shipping

  • Free return shipping for defective or wrong items
  • Customer responsible for return shipping on non-defective items
  • We recommend using tracked shipping for returns

How to Return

  1. Contact our support team at returns@akku.com.np
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Receive your Return Authorization (RA) number
  4. Pack items securely with RA number visible
  5. Ship to our returns center (address provided with RA)

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

Refund Policy

Our Refund Process

We process refunds promptly once your return has been received and inspected. Here's what you need to know:

Processing Time

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your return
  • Inspection takes 1-2 business days to verify item condition
  • Bank processing may take additional 3-5 business days

Refund Method

  • Refunds issued to original payment method
  • Credit/Debit card refunds: 5-10 business days
  • Digital wallet refunds (eSewa/Khalti): 2-3 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer or store credit

Refund Amount

  • Full refund for defective or wrong items (including shipping)
  • Product price only for customer-initiated returns
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable (except for defective items)
  • Return shipping costs deducted for non-defective items

Exchanges

  • We currently don't offer direct exchanges
  • Please return the item and place a new order
  • Priority processing for replacement orders

Store Credit Option

  • Receive instant store credit (no waiting period)
  • 10% bonus credit when choosing store credit over refund
  • Valid for 12 months from issue date
  • Can be used on any future purchase

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued if:

  • Item shows signs of use or wear
  • Missing original packaging or accessories
  • Item returned after 7-day window (at our discretion)

Questions about your refund? Contact us at support@akku.com.np or call +977 01-4123456
