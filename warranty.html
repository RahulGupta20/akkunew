Warranty Information

Understanding our warranty coverage and how to make warranty claims

Warranty Overview

At Akku Electronics, we stand behind the quality of our products. All items come with manufacturer warranties that protect your purchase against defects and ensure you have a reliable product.

Important Note

Warranty terms and duration vary by product category and manufacturer. Always check your product's warranty card and documentation for specific coverage details.

Standard Warranty Coverage

  • Duration: 1-2 Years manufacturer warranty (varies by brand)
  • Coverage: Manufacturing defects, hardware failures
  • What's Included: Parts replacement, labor costs, technical support
  • Brands: Dell, HP, Lenovo, ASUS, MSI, Acer
  • Service: On-site or carry-in service (brand-specific)
  • Duration: 1 Year official warranty from authorized distributors
  • Coverage: Manufacturing defects, hardware malfunctions
  • What's Included: Display issues, battery problems, charging issues, button malfunctions
  • Brands: Apple, Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus, Realme, OPPO, Vivo
  • Service Centers: Authorized service centers across major cities
  • Duration: 6 Months - 2 Years (product-specific)
  • Coverage: Manufacturing defects, component failures
  • Products: Keyboards, mice, headsets, webcams, microphones, speakers
  • Brands: Logitech, Razer, Corsair, HyperX, SteelSeries
  • Service: Replace or repair at our service center
  • Processors: 3 Years (Intel, AMD)
  • Motherboards: 3 Years
  • RAM: Lifetime warranty (most brands)
  • Graphics Cards: 2-3 Years
  • Power Supplies: 5-10 Years (varies by model)
  • Storage (SSD/HDD): 3-5 Years or TBW limit
  • Service: Direct RMA or replacement through Akku Electronics

How to Claim Warranty

Before making a warranty claim, ensure:

  • Your product is within the warranty period (check warranty card or invoice date)
  • The issue is covered under warranty terms
  • You have the original invoice and warranty card
  • The product has not been tampered with or modified

Reach out to us through:

  • Phone: +977 98123412345 (9 AM - 6 PM, Mon-Sat)
  • Email: warranty@akku.gg
  • Visit: Our showroom at Putalisadak, Kathmandu
  • Online: My Orders section > Request Warranty Service

Provide your order number, product details, and description of the issue.

Our technical team will:

  • Inspect the product for covered defects
  • Verify warranty eligibility
  • Assess whether repair or replacement is needed
  • Provide an estimated timeline for resolution

Processing Time: 24-48 hours for initial assessment

Depending on the issue:

  • Minor Issues: Same-day or next-day repair at our service center
  • Component Replacement: 3-7 business days
  • Full Replacement: 7-14 business days (subject to stock availability)
  • Manufacturer Service: 2-4 weeks (for brand-specific warranty)

We'll keep you updated throughout the process via SMS and email.

What's NOT Covered Under Warranty

  • Physical damage (drops, cracks, liquid damage)
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Damage from improper use or neglect
  • Normal wear and tear (scratches, fading)
  • Software issues, viruses, or operating system problems
  • Accessories like cables, adapters (unless explicitly covered)
  • Damage from power surges or electrical issues
  • Products with tampered or removed warranty seals
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality

Extended Warranty Plans

Get peace of mind with our extended warranty plans that go beyond manufacturer coverage:

Basic Extended Warranty

  • Duration: +1 Year beyond manufacturer warranty
  • Coverage: Hardware defects, component failures
  • Cost: 5-8% of product price
  • Available for: Laptops, desktops, mobile phones

Premium Extended Warranty

  • Duration: +2 Years beyond manufacturer warranty
  • Coverage: Hardware defects + accidental damage protection
  • Benefits: Priority service, free pickup & delivery, loaner device
  • Cost: 12-15% of product price
  • Available for: Premium laptops, gaming PCs, high-end phones

How to Purchase: Extended warranty plans can be added at the time of purchase or within 30 days of your original purchase date. Contact us to add extended warranty to your existing products.
